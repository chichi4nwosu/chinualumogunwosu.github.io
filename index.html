About Me

My name is Chinualumogu Nwosu, and I’m an Electrical and Computer Engineering student at The University of Texas at Austin. I’m passionate about exploring how innovation, design, and equity intersect—whether through engineering solutions, software development, or community-driven tech projects. My work spans creative coding, research, mentoring, and building things that matter, with a focus on accessibility, education, and impact.
Outside the classroom, I enjoy giving back through initiatives that uplift others, especially those historically underrepresented in STEM. From leading coding workshops to launching digital tools that educate and empower, I’m constantly seeking ways to bridge opportunity gaps and foster a more inclusive tech future. I'm excited for what lies ahead—and always open to learning, building, and connecting with those who share the vision of creating meaningful change.
