About Me

Hello, my name is Chinualumogu Nwosu, but you can call me Chi-Chi. I am an Electrical and Computer Engineering student at The University of Texas at Austin, expected to graduate in May 2029. I am also pursuing a business minor and hope to double major in mathematics.

On campus, I am involved in organizations such as IEEE, NSBE, Cockrell School Cares, Terry Scholars, Society of Women Engineers, and Texas Boxing Club, where I contribute to event planning, outreach, and building connections between students and opportunities.

Outside of academics, I enjoy collecting digital cameras, drawing, and organizing. I value both creativity and structure, and I am always looking for ways to grow while staying curious and engaged.
